# BodyTempGage — Privacy Policy

*Effective date: July 20, 2026 · [Русская версия](privacy-policy.ru.html)*

BodyTempGage (the phone and Wear OS apps, together "the App") reads temperature
broadcasts from Xiaomi Miaomiaoce MMC-T201 Bluetooth LE thermometers and shows them on
your device.

## What data the App handles

- **Temperature readings and thermometer battery level**, received over Bluetooth LE from
  a thermometer near you.
- **The Bluetooth identifier (MAC address) and name of the thermometer you select**, so the
  App can recognise it again.
- **Your settings**: display units, alert thresholds, monitoring options.

## Where the data goes

**Nowhere outside your own devices.** All data is processed and stored locally.
The App has no server, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs that
collect data. It never sends your data over the Internet.

If you use the Wear OS app, your settings (units, thresholds, selected thermometer)
are synchronised **between your own phone and watch** through the Google Play services
Wearable Data Layer. This sync happens within your paired devices.

Temperature history and settings may be included in your device's standard Android
backup (Google's backup service), governed by your device's backup settings and
Google's privacy policy.

## Permissions the App asks for

- **Bluetooth (Nearby devices)** — to scan for the thermometer's broadcasts. The
  thermometer is identified by its broadcast content; the App declares the scan as
  `neverForLocation` and does not derive location from it.
- **Location (only on Android 11 and older)** — required by Android itself for any
  Bluetooth LE scan on those versions. The App does not read, store, or transmit your
  location.
- **Notifications** — to show monitoring status and temperature alerts.

## Data retention and deletion

All data lives on your devices only. Uninstalling the App deletes everything it stored.

## Children

The App does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

## Not a medical device

BodyTempGage is not a medical device. The displayed body temperature is an estimate and
must not be used for diagnosis or treatment decisions.
